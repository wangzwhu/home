Zheng Wang (王正)

Project Researcher

I am currently a postdoctoral project researcher at Satoh-Lab, National Institute of Informatics (NII), Japan, supervised by Prof. Shin’ichi Satoh. I received my Ph.D. at National Engineering Research Center for Multimedia Software (NERCMS), Wuhan University in 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Ruimin Hu. My research interests focus on person re-identification and instance search.
